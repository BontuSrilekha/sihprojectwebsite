    Saving Lives of People in the Uttarakhand from bridge collapses

    Every year, Many bridges collapsed in Uttarakhand due to some factors. We're building a Bridge lifespan detection system to reduce the bridge collapses

    Get Started

    Our Objective

    A bridge is a construction marvel built to stretch a substantial hurdle such as body of water, road. Bridge collapses are often tragic but also alluring for the engineers. A number of bridges built over the physical obstacles deteriorate as these structures are experienced to external geomorphic conditions resulting in decrease in their lifespan.

    Get Started

    Main Motive

    The main motive of our project is to predict the bridge life span and send alert to authority which can be used to modify the design to increase the life of bridge. This is continuous monitoring system .Sensors are used to monitor bridge.

    About Us

    BAJRANGI is the system name, it means "Bridge-Life Assessment from Joint Referencing of Artificial Nodal Gadgets in India". Artificial nodal gadgets refer to several different sensors and mechanisms. ​ We, The team SPARKINNOVATORS are doing a joint reference to predict the lifespan of the bridge. We tried to establish the best possible ways to eradicate the bridge collapses. We find the life span of the bridge based on the geomorphic conditions, bridge design, and the parameters that effects the bridge and through the data, the Alert will be sent to the authorities then they will take necessary actions necessary precautions would be taken care by the authorities. ​ By developing a software system to predict the lifespan of the bridge, by using several geomorphic and environmental conditions, the system is advanced and robust.
